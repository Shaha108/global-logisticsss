ToshKargo – Urban Courier Logistics in Tashkent

1. Order Placement

Customers place orders via mobile app or website, entering pickup/drop-off info, item size, and urgency. This is the starting point of ToshKargo’s service flow.

2. Courier Assignment

Based on real-time location, traffic, and delivery urgency, a courier (mostly bike or scooter-based) is automatically assigned using smart dispatch software.

3. Pickup Process

The courier arrives at the pickup location, verifies package ID via QR code, checks packaging, and confirms readiness for delivery.

4. Urban Transit

The item is transported through optimized urban routes, often using bicycle lanes or less congested roads. Live tracking is available for the customer.

5. Micro-Hubs / Distribution Points

For longer deliveries or high-demand zones, ToshKargo uses small urban hubs to sort or temporarily hold items for efficient re-routing.

6. Final Delivery (Last Mile)

The courier delivers the item directly to the customer's address, confirms receipt, and obtains a digital signature or photo proof.

7. Quality Control

Each courier’s performance is rated. Delivery speed, item condition, and customer satisfaction are monitored daily via the internal dashboard.

8. After-Sales Support

Dedicated agents handle inquiries, resolve delivery issues, and offer refund or redelivery options. Sustainability: ToshKargo supports reusable bags and offers green delivery options.

9. Logistics & Sustainability Management

70% of deliveries use zero-emission vehicles. AI tools optimize delivery routes to reduce fuel and time. Paperless systems and recycling initiatives support Tashkent’s green goals.
