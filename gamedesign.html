1. Empathize

Understand your players' needs, motivations, and pain points

  • Player research: Interviews, surveys, observation
  • Player personas: Create archetypes of your target audience
  • Emotional mapping: Identify desired emotional journey
  • Competitor analysis: Study similar games' strengths/weaknesses

2. Define

Clearly articulate the core player experience and design challenges

  • Problem statements: "Players need [X] because [Y]"
  • Core pillars: Define 3-5 foundational game concepts
  • Player goals: What players should feel/achieve
  • Constraints: Technical, budgetary, time limitations

3. Ideate

Generate creative solutions and game concepts

  • Mechanics mapping: How systems interact
  • Paper prototyping: Quick physical mockups
  • Feature prioritization: Must-have vs nice-to-have

4. Prototype

Build tangible representations of your ideas

  • Grayboxing: Basic geometry testing
  • Wizard of Oz: Fake backend with human control
  • Modular approach: Build/test systems independently
