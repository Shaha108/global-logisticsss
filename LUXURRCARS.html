About LUXURY LIFE

LUXURY LIFE is a premium car rental service website based in Busan, South Korea. The project aims to provide an elegant platform for customers to rent luxury vehicles with pricing ranging from ₩200,000 to ₩450,000.

Key Features

  • Premium luxury vehicle rentals in Busan
  • Competitive pricing from ₩200,000 - ₩450,000
  • Professional brand identity
  • Direct contact system (+8210 1234 5678)
  • Clean, minimalist design approach
  • Mobile-responsive layout

Target Audience

High-end customers seeking luxury transportation solutions for business, special events, tourism, and premium lifestyle experiences in Busan metropolitan area.

⚠️ Problems & Solutions ▼

Problem: Limited Visual Content

Issue: No vehicle images displayed on the website

Solution: Implement high-quality car photography gallery with professional vehicle showcases, 360° views, and interior/exterior image galleries.

Problem: Basic Functionality

Issue: No booking system or interactive features

Solution: Develop online reservation system with calendar integration, availability checking, and automated booking confirmation.

Problem: Content Depth

Issue: Minimal information about services and vehicles

Solution: Create detailed vehicle descriptions, terms of service, FAQ section, and comprehensive service information pages.

Problem: User Experience

Issue: Limited navigation and site structure

Solution: Implement multi-page layout with intuitive navigation menu, breadcrumbs, and improved user journey flow.
